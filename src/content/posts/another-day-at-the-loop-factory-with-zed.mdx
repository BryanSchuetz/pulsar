My timelines these days are chockablock of equal parts doomers, accelerationists, and scouts. Takes range from 'AI is a fascists tool that will steal your job and destroy Democracy' to 'Post scarcity is just around the corner'. My own perspective lies somewhere between those two extremes—I'd like to think of it as reasonable and pragmantic, where not hopelessly overburdened by my own priors.

The fact is that I've been getting paid to type machine readable/translatable characters into text editors (in the service of building useful tools) for a long time now.  Things have changed a lot in that time—I remember the vibe shift when CSS dropped—but I've never experienced a step-change like this.
